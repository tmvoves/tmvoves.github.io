Pantry Essentials

Pantry like a pro. The difference between a “meh” weeknight dinner and a memorable one? A kitchen stocked with powerhouse ingredients you’ll reach for again and again. These days that means anything from punchy spice blends to flavor-packed condiments

Matcha

Matcha can be mixed with water or milk or used for baked goods. Since Matcha is the whole leaf ground is is a great source of chlorophyll and amino acid L-Theanine. Unlike coffee, matcha produces a calm feeling as well as a caffeine jolt.

Jacobsen Sea Salt

Used by professionals across the country. Use their salt to amplify your dishes and build flavor in your food. Jacobsen is the first company to harvest sea salt in the Pacific Northwest since Lewis and Clark.

Buy Now

Tahini

Tahini is made of toasted sesame seeds. It is both savory and nutty and can be used as a replacement for peanut butter. It can be used in salad dressings or swirled into brownies.

Buy Now

Sambal

A sweet, vinegary hot sauce that will spice up any meal. Use it in curries or on top of your pizza to add a more diverse flavor profile to your meals. Once you try it, it will become an essential item in your pantry and an easy way to spice up a bland office meal.

Buy Now

Miso

Yes, it;s fundamental for miso soup, but it also gives instant depth to sauces and marinades. red is more robust than yellow or white.

Buy Now

Coconut Oil

we like the refined version of this rich oil for sauteing because it has a higher smoke point, for more coconutty flabor go with virgin.

Buy Now

Turmeric

The ground form of this flammation reducing rhizone has been used as a seasoning, herbal remedy, and fabric dye fur millennials. It's sweet, warming, and slightly bitter. Add it to the aromatics when makinf curries, vegetable soups, and rice pilafs.

Furikake

These savory sprinkles are supercharged with seaweed, fish flakes, sugar, salt, and sesame. Let it rain on fried rice, grain bowls, or crispyskinned salmon fillets.

Buy Now

Nutrional Yeast

Vegans love this magic dust because it's a great dairy-free parmesan stand-in. that may be where nutritional yeast got its flower power reputation but don't knock it till you try it. Its umami-ness makes bowls of popcorn more addictive and roasted vegetables more complex.
