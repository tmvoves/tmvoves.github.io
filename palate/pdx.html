Portland City guide

Joshua McFadden reveals his favorite places to eat in Portland.

Top 5 Spots

2.

Uno Mas
Vibrant taqueria featuring an extensive choice of fillings with outdoor seating.
*Chicharrón Tacos

1.

Little Bird Bistro
Eclectic French bistro fare served in a modern spot with red leather booths.
*Le Pigeon Burger

3.

Pok Pok
Authentic Thai street food & unique cocktails served at a quaint, modern spot.
*Neua Naam Tok

4.

Woodsman Tavern
Northwest inspired small plates & platters, plus inventive cocktails, served in a cavernous space with rustic vibe.
*Ham Plate

5.

Luce
Petite, low-key Italian restaurant and grocery serving housemade pastas & seasonal entrees.
*Pasta Vongole

Joshua's Restaurants

Tusk

Israeli food is in the midst of a renaissance in the states, and Tusk is at the forefront. Make room for next-level mezze, and delight in the vibrancy of flavor and color.

Ava Gene's

 The family style meal here is an experience for the books — think Roman-inspired hit after hit from chef Joshua MacFadden’s repertoire combined with a seductive, trattoria-like energy.
